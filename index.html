Supporting Students Across the UK

At Zen Garden, we believe that managing rent shouldn't be a source of stress for students. We founded this platform to bridge the gap between student finances and landlord requirements, making accommodation payments simple and predictable.

Our mission is to empower students to focus on their education by removing the anxiety around rent payments. With our flexible payment plans and intuitive platform, you can stay on top of your housing costs without the worry.

We work directly with student accommodation providers and private landlords across the UK to ensure seamless payment processing and clear communication for all parties.
